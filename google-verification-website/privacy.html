Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

Information We Collect

This Google integration tool does not store, collect, or process your personal information directly. All data interactions occur through Google's secure APIs using OAuth 2.0 authentication. We only access the Google services you explicitly authorize us to access.

How We Use Information

When you authorize this application to access your Google services, we only use this access to perform the specific functions you request, such as:

  • Reading emails to provide summaries or extract information
  • Checking calendar events to schedule or manage appointments
  • Accessing Drive files to organize or retrieve documents

All processing happens in real-time and no copies of your data are stored permanently.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information to any third parties. All data remains within Google's ecosystem and is accessed only temporarily during requested operations.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your data. All connections to Google services are encrypted using HTTPS and OAuth 2.0 protocols.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we access through Google APIs
  • Request deletion of your data from our temporary caches
  • Revoke our access to your Google services at any time through your Google Account settings
  • Contact us with questions about your privacy rights

OpenClaw Ecosystem Integration

This project integrates seamlessly with the OpenClaw ecosystem. Any data processed through this integration remains within your local OpenClaw environment and is not transmitted to external services beyond the required Google API calls.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
