Google Services Integration for OpenClaw

Secure and efficient Google services integration for OpenClaw-based AI assistants

About This Integration Service

This is a personal project by cheshirecode designed to provide secure integration with Google services including Gmail, Google Calendar, and Google Drive for OpenClaw-based AI assistants. The tools enable authorized access to your Google data to enhance productivity and automation while maintaining the highest security standards.

How It Works

The Google integration follows Google's OAuth 2.0 protocol to securely connect with your Google account. We request only the minimum permissions necessary to perform the requested functions, such as reading emails, accessing calendar events, or managing drive files based on your explicit requests.

Security & Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. Your credentials are never stored, and all access is handled through Google's secure OAuth system. You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings.

OpenClaw Ecosystem Integration

This project integrates seamlessly with the OpenClaw ecosystem, enhancing the capabilities of AI assistants by providing secure access to Google services. The integration is designed to work within the OpenClaw framework while respecting user privacy and security.
